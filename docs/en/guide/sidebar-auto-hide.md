# Sidebar Auto-hide

Want a more immersive chat experience?

We provide a **Sidebar Auto-hide** feature. When enabled, the sidebar automatically collapses when your mouse leaves the sidebar area, and automatically expands when you move your mouse back into it.

### How to Enable

1. Open the Gemini Voyager settings panel.
2. Find the **Auto-hide sidebar** toggle in **General Settings**.
3. Toggle it on to activate.

_Note: This feature currently only supports Google Gemini._
